## 4. Content Patterns & Insights  


### Formats That Perform Best  


1. Listicles with local city focus — Average Conversion Rate: 2.8%  
2. Trend-driven thought leadership — Average Engagement Rate: 5%  
3. Short aspirational social posts — Average Engagement Rate: 4–6%  


---


### Topics That Resonate  


1. Flexibility vs. rigid contracts — consistently drives conversions  
2. Multi-city lifestyle — consistently drives engagement  
3. Studio growth & occupancy insights — consistently drives partner inquiries  


---


### Headlines That Work  


- Pattern: "[Number] Ways to Stay Fit Without a Long-Term Contract"  
- Pattern: "How [Studio Name] Increased Weekday Occupancy by [X]%"  
- Pattern: "The Flexible Fitness Guide for Urban Professionals"  


---


### Optimal Publishing  


- **Best days:** Tuesday and Thursday  
- **Best times:** 8–10 AM local time  
- **Ideal length:** Blog: 1,200–1,500 words | Social: 80–150 words  


---


## 5. Lessons from Underperforming Content  


### What Doesn't Work  


- Overly promotional tone without educational value  
- Generic fitness advice not tied to urban context  
- Long-form articles without clear headers or structure  


---


### Content to Avoid  


- Broad “Top 10 Fitness Tips” without MoveAtlas positioning (low differentiation)  
- Highly technical industry reports without clear member benefit (low engagement)
